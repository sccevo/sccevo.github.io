sc-cEvo Software

Welcome! We are a subgroup of the Computational Evolution group at the Department of Biosystems Science and Engineering at ETH Zurich. We develop computational methods for studying development at single-cell resolution.

Lineage Tracing System Editing Models

BEAST2 packages for reconstructing phylogenies from lineage tracing data originating from diverse experimental systems.

Package Type of target sites Example lineage tracing systems
TiDeTree Independent target sites intMEMOIR, ScarTrace, LINNAEUS, iTracer
GABI Dependent target sites GESTALT
SciPhy Sequential target sites DNA Typewriter, peCHYRON

Phylodynamic Models

  • ADB (Age-Dependent Branching model) - BEAST2 package for quantifying cell population dynamics based on phylogenies.
